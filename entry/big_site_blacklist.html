Big Site Blacklist

Don't you hate it when you search for a song, a book, or an anime, and google give you some lame sites in results that either do not contain what you are looking for at all, either contain but in very handicaped state, like book being cut to first chapter or split to pages, either require you to do some nasty things, like watching ads for minute to get what you want?

Rejoice, no more that!

Here is the blacklist

To use it, install the cjs plugin, or anything similar if you are not under chrome (like greasemonkey i suppose)

And add the "https://cdn.rawgit.com/klesun/shmidusic.lv/master/scripts/cjs/google.com.js" to "your own external scripts" in the plugin when you are on the google page

To contribute, add an issue on the github
